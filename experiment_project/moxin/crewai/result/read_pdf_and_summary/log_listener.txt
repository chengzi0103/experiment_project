Crewai Agent Result: The author of the article is Zhentao Xu. The time is 6 May 2024. The main goal of the article is to propose a new approach in customer service question answering that overcomes limitations related to the loss of vital information when compressing documents into text chunks and reduced answer quality from segmentation. The proposed approach parses issue tickets into trees and connects individual issue tickets to form an interconnected graph, preserving the logical coherence of ticket sections and achieving high retrieval performance.
